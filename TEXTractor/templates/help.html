About TEXTractor

TEXTractor is a pain scoring tool for clinical reports.

CONTROLS

  • ACCESS KEY: You need access key to log in to the app
  • INFO: Opens the help page in a new tab
  • PREVIOUS: Loads the previous patient
  • NEXT: Loads the next patient
  • SAVE: Saves the selected points
  • Scoreboard: The selected pain score will be added to the scoreboard.
  • Click on a note in the scoreboard to load it.

    • *

    PAIN Scoring Guideline:

  • Use the  N/A  option only if the pain is not mentioned in the note or the pain term is in hypothetical, conditional, or historical sentences and does not reflect the current state of the pain. 
  • Use none if the patient is comfortable and explicitly denied having pain 
  • Report physician reported pain score (mild, moderate, severe) for the notes with valid mentions of pain according to the following verbal rating scale (VRS). Use your best judgment or assumption if the pain score is not clearly mentioned in the report.
  • IMPORTANT NOTE: Pain score should reflect the current stage of the pain (pain score at the time of the visit or hospitalization). So, any mention of the pan in a historical, hypothetical or conditional sentence should be ignored.
